LIONEL MESSI

About THE GOAT

Lionel Andrés Messi is a legendary Argentine professional footballer, universally considered one of the greatest players in the history of the sport. Known for his masterful dribbling, playmaking vision, and prolific goal-scoring, Messi spent the prime of his club career at FC Barcelona, where he became the club's all-time leading scorer and won countless trophies. His unparalleled individual success is highlighted by a record eight Ballon d'Or awards. He cemented his GOAT status by captaining the Argentina national team to victory in both the 2021 Copa América and the 2022 FIFA World Cup
